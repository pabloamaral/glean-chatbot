OVERVIEW

Banks & Banjo LLC is organized into five core divisions, each led by a member of the Executive Leadership Team (ELT). This document provides an overview of each division, its key teams, and the right contacts for common cross-functional needs.

Total headcount as of February 2025: ~320 employees across 4 offices (San Francisco HQ, New York, London, and Austin) plus a distributed remote workforce.

DIVISIONS & TEAMS

1. ENGINEERING & PRODUCT (Reports to CTO: Priya Nair)

Teams:
- Platform Engineering: Core infrastructure, reliability, and developer tooling. Lead: Ravi Gupta.
- Product Engineering: Customer-facing features and integrations. Lead: Fatima Al-Rashidi.
- Data & ML: Data pipelines, analytics infrastructure, and machine learning models. Lead: Jonah Park.
- Product Management: Product strategy, roadmap, and prioritization. Lead: Sarah Bloom.
- Design & UX: Product design, design systems, and user research. Lead: Alex Torres.

Key contacts:
- Engineering oncall / incidents: #oncall-eng (Slack) or pagerduty@banksandbanjo.com
- Bug reports (internal): #bugs (Slack)
- Product roadmap questions: Sarah Bloom or your product partner

2. SALES & REVENUE (Reports to CRO: Tom Ellison)

Teams:
- Enterprise Sales: New logo acquisition for accounts >500 employees. Lead: Carla Voss.
- Mid-Market Sales: Accounts 50–500 employees. Lead: Derek Huang.
- Sales Development (SDR): Pipeline generation and outbound. Lead: Priya Menon.
- Revenue Operations: CRM, tooling, forecasting, and commissions. Lead: Brian Osei.

Key contacts:
- CRM (Salesforce) issues: Revenue Ops via #revops (Slack)
- Commission questions: Brian Osei or payroll@banksandbanjo.com
- Customer introductions / referrals: Your account executive or Carla Voss

3. CUSTOMER SUCCESS & SUPPORT (Reports to COO: James Whitfield)

Teams:
- Customer Success Managers (CSMs): Ongoing customer relationships, renewals, and expansion.
- Technical Support: Tier 1 and Tier 2 customer issue resolution.
- Professional Services: Implementation, onboarding, and custom solutions for enterprise customers.
- Solutions Architecture: Pre-sales and post-sales technical guidance. Lead: Rachel Kim.

Key contacts:
- Customer escalations: #customer-escalations (Slack)
- Internal product feedback from customers: #customer-feedback (Slack)
- Professional services scoping: Rachel Kim

4. FINANCE & LEGAL (Reports to CFO: David Chen)

Teams:
- FP&A: Budgeting, forecasting, and financial reporting.
- Accounting: Accounts payable, receivable, and month-end close.
- Legal & Compliance: Contracts, employment law, and regulatory matters.

Key contacts:
- Expense reimbursements: expenses@banksandbanjo.com or Workday
- Contract reviews (vendor/customer): legal@banksandbanjo.com
- Budget questions: Your department's FP&A partner
- Invoices / AP: accounting@banksandbanjo.com

5. PEOPLE & OPERATIONS (Reports to CPO: Maria Santos)

Teams:
- Talent Acquisition: Recruiting and hiring. Lead: Nina Powell.
- People Operations (HR): Employee lifecycle, benefits, policies. Lead: Chris Yamamoto.
- Workplace & IT: Office management, IT infrastructure, equipment. Lead: Omar Farooq.
- Learning & Development: Training programs, manager development, onboarding.

Key contacts:
- HR/People questions: people@banksandbanjo.com or #hr-questions (Slack)
- Recruiting (referrals, open roles): Nina Powell or careers@banksandbanjo.com
- IT issues: it-help@banksandbanjo.com or #it-support (Slack)
- Office/facilities: facilities@banksandbanjo.com
